Just the FAQ's about SUMSRI

Is Miami University in Florida? No. Miami University is in Oxford, Ohio and actually existed before Miami, Florida! Oxford is located in the rolling hills of southwestern Ohio. The summer's are comfortable with cool mornings and evenings and only a few days per summer in the 90's. Our dormitory and classrooms are air-conditioned.

Click here for Miami University's home page.  Click here for information on Oxford, Ohio.
Will I be teamed one-to-one with a Miami professor to do research? No. Each student will be assigned to one of three research groups based on the student's personal choice and mathematics background. Each group has a director. Two of these directors will be Miami professors. The third director will be brought in from another university. The research director will suggest topics for study. Groups range in size from 3 to 7 students. Students are encouraged to be part of a research team.
Can I get academic credit for attending SUMSRI? SUMSRI may count as research credit at your college upon application.  Check into it.  At this time Miami University does not offer credit for SUMSRI..  
Will I have access to a computer while attending SUMSRI? Yes. All SUMSRI participants have access to two computer labs in the Mathematics Department. Each dormitory room will also be equipped with a computer. Each computer contains the software packages needed to do your assignments and research, including LaTex mathematical typesetting package.
What will our schedule be like? The schedule for SUMSRI has not been set yet. Classes will vary based on the availability of the professors. Basically the first three weeks are pretty structured and look something like this:

The last four weeks are unstructured time in which to conduct further research and meet with your seminar director on writing up the results of your research and preparing a final presentation.
Will we have to find our own housing? No. SUMSRI students are housed together in an air-conditioned dormitory on campus. Meals are provided in one of the campus cafeterias. With "UpTown Bucks' cards to purchase meals in Oxford.  There is a small kitchen available for common use in the dormitory.

SUMSRI students liked to study in the dormitory hallway! But this is not a requirement of the program.
What recreational facilities are available at Miami University? Miami University has a fully equipped Recreational Sports Center including an Olympic-size swimming pool and diving platforms, weight rooms, indoor track, climbing wall and equipment rental. We have tennis courts and Goggin Ice Arena on campus. Beautiful jogging trails are also nearby.
What should I bring to SUMSRI?
  • Comfortable clothes and personal grooming items 
  • A fall weight jacket (the mornings and some rooms can be chilly) 
  • Comfortable shoes for walking across campus and into town. (there is no public transportation during the summer, but most things are available within a square mile) 
  • bed linens.
Can I drive my car to SUMSRI? Yes.  Please let the Program Coordinator know you'll have a car on campus.  Parking permits are necessary.
